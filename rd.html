R&D

The researchers at Phoenix Robotics LLC ultimately have one shared vision: to help enable the commoditization of robots so that anyone can find them useful on a daily basis. Our in-house research is thus focused on this theme to develop a unique value proposition. While we cannot reveal much publicly in the present moment, we are excited to share our progress with those who have serious interest in partnering with us. We have big plans and would like to find like-minded folks who share our vision.

Please feel welcome to contact us and learn more about our transformational research.
